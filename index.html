Fit rules for your body

Keep only if

  • Shoulder seam sits exactly on your shoulder bone.
  • Body has a little space, not clingy and not floating.
  • Sleeves end around mid-bicep for tees/polos.
  • Trouser leg is slim-straight, not skinny.
  • Tailored trousers are a matte fabric — shiny reads too formal for a casual office.
  • Shoe shape is low-profile and clean.

Return if

  • Shoulder drops past your arm. This kills the look.
  • Fabric is shiny, thin, transparent, or clingy.
  • Overshirt makes you look smaller.
  • Trousers bunch hard at the shoe.
  • Logo feels loud or sporty.
