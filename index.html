How to pick & wear

Keep only if

Shoulder seam sits on your shoulder bone · body has a little room (not clingy, not floating) · trousers slim-straight not skinny · tailored trousers are MATTE not shiny · sneakers low-profile.

Return if

Shoulder drops past your arm · fabric shiny/thin/clingy · it swamps your frame · jeans washed/whiskered/distressed · sneaker chunky · logo loud.

Best capsule = ~2 polos + 1 knit + 1 overshirt + 2 trousers/chino + 1 dark jean + 1 leather sneaker + 1 heavy tee, all in one tight palette (e.g. charcoal/navy/olive/stone). Verified via browser; stock & prices change.
